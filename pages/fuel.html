About Fuel Prices

Fuel prices in India are revised daily based on the global crude oil prices. The prices vary from state to state due to different local taxes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How are fuel prices determined?
Fuel prices are determined based on the average price of crude oil in the international market and the exchange rate of the Indian rupee against the US dollar.
Why do fuel prices vary across states?
Fuel prices vary across states due to differences in state-level taxes, such as Value Added Tax (VAT).
How can I check the latest fuel prices?
You can use our Fuel Price Lookup tool to check the latest petrol and diesel prices in your city.
